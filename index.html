How to Use the QR Code Generator

Convert any text, URL, or password into a scannable QR code instantly — 100% in your browser, nothing uploaded to any server.

  • Enter any URL, WiFi password, or text in the input field
  • Click "Use Password" to instantly QR-code your generated password
